AluPanels provides a wide Engineering Service Range including Technical & Financial Proposals, structural calculations, Shop Drawing, Concept Designs, & Material allocation.

How can we help?

We provide a comprehensive, end-to-end suite of engineering services designed to guide your project from initial vision to final implementation. Our expertise begins with innovative Concept Designs that transform your ideas into feasible plans, supported by detailed Technical & Financial Proposals for clear budgeting and project scope. We then deliver precise structural calculations to ensure absolute integrity, safety, and compliance with all regulations.

This technical foundation is brought to life through meticulous Shop Drawings, providing fabricators and contractors with crystal-clear instructions for accurate construction. Finally, we optimize your project's efficiency and cost-effectiveness through strategic Material Allocation, selecting the right materials in the right quantities.
