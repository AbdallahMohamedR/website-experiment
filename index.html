Our Products

Curtain Walls & Skylights

AluPanels used all the Profiles found in the local market whether it’s a Conventional or Structural Glazing System. Providing a wide range of profiles from 50 mm up to 200 mm length.

AluPanels has executed skylights with various specs whether they are straight, inclined, domes, or Pyramid shapes achieving the best acoustic, and weather sealing.

Aluminum Windows & Doors

AluPanels used a wide range of products found in local market achieving different specs to meet the performances needed. sliding systems, Hinged Doors thermal, and non-thermal profiles to achieve client's requires

Aluminum Composite Panels

AluPanels Do all ACP Processes as cutting , Bending , Perforation via CNC Routers , to all Kind of ACP Cladding 4mm & 6 mm whether it’s Normal PE, Fire Rated A2 or Fire Rated B1 , With Variant sub frames Steel or Alum whether it's single chasis , Double chasis even heavy steel structure main frames According to client Requires & structure Calculation sheets.

Aluminum Louvers

AluPanels used wide range of products extends from Box louvers, Z blades, Elliptical ,Sand trap, and bird screen louvers.
